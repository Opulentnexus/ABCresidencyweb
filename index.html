Kerala’s Hidden Wonders

Vagamon

Vagamon

Misty hills, pine forests, tea plantations, trekking spots and breathtaking valleys make Vagamon one of Kerala’s most peaceful hill stations.

Thekkady

Thekkady

Explore wildlife sanctuary, Periyar Tiger Reserve, boating experiences and rich forest biodiversity.

Charlie Kulam

Charlie Kulam

Charlie Kulam is a scenic monsoon pond in Peermade, Idukki, famous as a filming location of the Malayalam movie Charlie. Surrounded by tea estates and misty hills, it is a popular spot for sightseeing and photography.

Panchalimedu

Panchalimedu

Rolling green hills, cool climate and panoramic viewpoints create an unforgettable mountain experience.

Parunthumpara

Parunthumpara

Famous for dramatic cliffs, foggy landscapes and breathtaking sunrise viewpoints.

Kuttikkanam

Kuttikkanam
